## üéØ EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### **Subject Line Rules:**
‚úì Keep under 60 characters
‚úì Include company name + key metric/descriptor
‚úì Create curiosity or urgency
‚úì Avoid spam words ("free," "guaranteed," etc.)

### **Body Content Rules:**
‚úì Under 200 words (VCs get 100+ emails/day)
‚úì Clear problem/solution/traction structure
‚úì Specific numbers (not "many" or "lots")
‚úì ONE clear call-to-action
‚úì Include social proof when possible

### **Timing Strategy:**
- **Initial email:** Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM (their timezone)
- **Follow-up #1:** 5 business days later
- **Follow-up #2:** 10 business days after Follow-up #1
- **Follow-up #3 (final):** 14 business days after Follow-up #2
- **Max follow-ups:** 3 (don't be annoying)

### **Personalization Checklist:**
‚ñ° Use their first name
‚ñ° Reference their portfolio company (relevant one)
‚ñ° Mention their investment thesis/focus area
‚ñ° Show you've done research (not copy-paste)
‚ñ° Adapt tone to their firm culture (formal vs casual)

## üß™ A/B TESTING RECOMMENDATIONS

**Test these variables:**
1. **Subject lines:** AWS comparison vs Problem-focused vs Metrics-focused
2. **Length:** 150 words vs 250 words
3. **CTA:** "Available for a call?" vs "Can I send our deck?" vs "Want API credentials?"
4. **Urgency:** Mention other investors vs Don't mention
5. **Social proof:** Include press/partners vs Don't include

**Track metrics:**
- Open rate (target: 30-40%)
- Response rate (target: 10-15%)
- Meeting conversion (target: 50% of responses)

---

## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Warm intros = 10x better** than cold emails. Always try to find a connection first.

2. **Send from your personal domain** (not Gmail if possible) - better deliverability.

3. **No attachments** in cold emails (spam filters). Link to deck instead.

4. **Mobile-optimized:** 60% of VCs read email on phone. Keep paragraphs short.

5. **Time zone awareness:** Send emails to arrive 9-10 AM in their timezone.

6. **Follow-up persistence:** 80% of deals require 5+ touchpoints. Don't give up early.

7. **Track everything:** Use a CRM or spreadsheet to track each outreach (dates, responses, next steps).

8. **Learn from replies:** If someone says "not right now," ask why. Feedback is gold.
