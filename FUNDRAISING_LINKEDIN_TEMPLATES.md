## üìä LINKEDIN STRATEGY BEST PRACTICES

### **Connection Request Rules:**
‚úì Personalize every request (mention their portfolio/interests)
‚úì Keep under 300 characters (LinkedIn limit)
‚úì Include reason for connecting
‚úì Don't pitch in connection request - just connect
‚úì Connect with 10-15 investors per day (don't spam)

### **Direct Message Rules:**
‚úì Wait 24-48 hours after connection accepted
‚úì Thank them for connecting first
‚úì Keep first message under 150 words
‚úì Include clear call-to-action
‚úì Always offer value (demo, insights, credentials)

### **Follow-Up Strategy:**
- **Message #1:** Introduction + ask for meeting
- **Wait 5 days**
- **Message #2:** Update + reiterate value
- **Wait 10 days**
- **Message #3 (final):** Last check-in, give exit opportunity

### **InMail Best Practices:**
‚úì Use InMail only if they're not accepting connections
‚úì Subject line = mini-pitch (under 60 chars)
‚úì First paragraph = hook (problem/solution)
‚úì Include social proof (traction, press, partners)
‚úì Clear CTA at the end

## üéØ TARGETING STRATEGY

### **Priority Order:**

3. **Active on LinkedIn** (posts regularly) - more likely to respond

### **Red Flags to Avoid:**

## üí° PRO TIPS

1. **Build your profile first:** Make sure YOUR LinkedIn profile looks professional before reaching out. Include HUMANBIBLIO in your headline, add demo video to featured section.

2. **Engage before pitching:** Like and comment on their posts for 1-2 weeks before connecting. They'll recognize your name.

3. **Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator:** If you can afford it ($99/mo), it makes finding and tracking VCs much easier.

4. **Post about your journey:** Share your fundraising journey publicly. VCs often discover companies via their feed.

5. **Tag relevant people:** When you post about HUMANBIBLIO, tag relevant VCs (tastefully). Increases visibility.

6. **Video messages:** LinkedIn allows video messages. Consider sending 30-second video pitch to stand out.

7. **Mutual connections:** If you have a mutual connection, ask for a warm intro instead of cold outreach (10x better).

8. **Response time matters:** Respond within 2 hours if they reply. Shows you're serious and organized.
