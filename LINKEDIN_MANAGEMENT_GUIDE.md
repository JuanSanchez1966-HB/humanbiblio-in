## üéØ THE GOLDEN RULES

1. **Consistency > Perfection**
   - Better to post regularly (2-3x/week) than perfectly (1x/month)

2. **Value > Promotion**
   - 80% valuable content, 20% promotional

3. **Engagement > Broadcasting**
   - Respond to every comment, especially in first 2 hours

4. **Authenticity > Polished**
   - Share struggles, not just wins
   - Be human, not corporate

5. **Long-term > Viral**
   - Build real relationships, not just follower counts
   - One investor connection > 1,000 random followers

6. **Quality > Quantity**
   - 500 targeted connections > 5,000 random ones
   - 10 thoughtful comments > 100 "Great post!"s
