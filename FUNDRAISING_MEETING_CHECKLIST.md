## üí° FINAL PRO TIPS

1. **Control the narrative:** Don't let them derail you. Answer questions but bring it back to your key points.

2. **Show confidence but not arrogance:** You're solving a real problem. Own it. But stay humble and coachable.

3. **Ask questions too:** Fundraising is two-way. You're evaluating them as much as they're evaluating you.

4. **Don't bad-mouth competitors:** LinkedIn, Tinder, OpenAI are successful companies. Respect them, but show how you're different.

5. **Be honest about unknowns:** If you don't know something, say "Great question, I don't have that data yet but I'll get it to you by [date]."

6. **Energy matters:** VCs invest in people they believe in. Show passion, conviction, and energy.

7. **Follow up relentlessly:** 80% of term sheets come after 5+ touchpoints. Don't give up after one meeting.

8. **Create urgency (if true):** If you have other interested investors, mention it. FOMO works on VCs too.
