## PHASE 4: TERM SHEET & CLOSING (Weeks 10-12)

### 4.1 Term Sheet Negotiation

**Key Terms to Negotiate:**

1. **Valuation**
   - Target: $10M post-money (15% equity for $1.5M)
   - Floor: $8M post-money (18.75% equity)
   - Ceiling: $12M post-money (12.5% equity)

2. **Liquidation Preference**
   - Target: 1x non-participating
   - Avoid: Participating preferred (double-dip)

3. **Board Seats**
   - Offer: 1 investor board seat (out of 3-5 total)
   - Retain: Founder majority (2 founder seats)

4. **Pro-Rata Rights**
   - Acceptable: Investors can maintain % in future rounds
   - Standard: Yes, up to their ownership percentage

5. **Vesting**
   - Standard: 4-year vest, 1-year cliff for founders
   - Negotiable: Accelerated vesting on acquisition/IPO

6. **Anti-Dilution**
   - Target: Broad-based weighted average
   - Avoid: Full ratchet (too investor-friendly)

**Red Flags to Avoid:**
- ‚ùå >2x liquidation preference
- ‚ùå Participating preferred stock
- ‚ùå Founder vesting <4 years
- ‚ùå Super pro-rata rights (investors can buy >100% of future rounds)
- ‚ùå Excessive board control (2+ investor seats at seed stage)

**Lawyer Recommendation:**
- Hire startup lawyer for term sheet review
