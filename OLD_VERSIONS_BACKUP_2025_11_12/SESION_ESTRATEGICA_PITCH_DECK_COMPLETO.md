## LESSONS LEARNED (Para Futuros Founders)

### **1. Start Smaller Than You Think**
**Original plan:** Toronto 35K users Year 1 = too ambitious
**Revised plan:** Niagara 3,150 users 18 months = achievable

**Lesson:** Geographic density > spread. Dominate one city before scaling.

**Precedents:**
- Nextdoor: 1 neighborhood (2011) ‚Üí 100 neighborhoods (2013) ‚Üí national (2015)
- Uber: San Francisco (2010) ‚Üí 1 year to add NYC
- DoorDash: Palo Alto (2013) ‚Üí 2 years to scale beyond

---

### **2. Price for Sustainability, Not Growth**
**Original plan:** $5/mo = rapid adoption but unsustainable (22-month payback)
**Revised plan:** $19/mo = slower but sustainable (2.3-month payback)

**Lesson:** LTV:CAC ratio > user count. Better to have 1,000 profitable users than 10,000 unprofitable.

**Precedents:**
- Slack: Started $8/user/month (2014), raised to $15/user (2020) = early users churned
- Better: Charge sustainable price Day 1

---

### **3. Solve Cold Start BEFORE Launch**
**Original plan:** Launch TrustScore, hope users build scores organically
**Revised plan:** 4-prong cold start (Google import, endorsements, verification, Founding Member)

**Lesson:** Chicken-egg problems kill platforms. Solve with hybrid approach (import existing data + incentivize early users).

**Precedents:**
- Yelp: Imported Yellow Pages data (didn't wait for user reviews)
- LinkedIn: Imported address books (instant network)
- Airbnb: Cross-posted to Craigslist (borrowed existing demand)

---

### **4. Outsource Non-Core Infrastructure**
**Original plan:** Build escrow in-house (legal, compliance, engineering)
**Revised plan:** Stripe Connect ($0 setup, 2.9% fee)

**Lesson:** Don't build what you can buy. Stripe spent 10 years + $1B+ on payments infrastructure. Use it.

**Precedents:**
- Every modern marketplace: Uber, Airbnb, TaskRabbit = use Stripe
- In-house only makes sense at massive scale (Amazon, PayPal)

---

### **5. Anti-Burnout is Non-Negotiable**
**Original plan:** Founders work 80-hour weeks, sacrifice everything
**Revised plan:** Founder salary Month 4, 18-month timeline, clear delegation

**Lesson:** Burnout kills more startups than competition. Sustainable pace > heroic sprints.

**Precedents:**
- Arianna Huffington (collapsed from exhaustion, now preaches sustainability)
- Rand Fishkin (Moz): "My biggest regret was not taking care of my mental health"
